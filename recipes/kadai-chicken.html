Kadai chicken

Description:

This is one of the delicious and easiest chicken dishes I have ever made. You can increase or decrease the chile peppers according to your taste.

Ingredients:

  • 5 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 7 dried red chile peppers, finely chopped
  • 5 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 4 boneless chicken breasts, cut into bite-size pieces

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add chile peppers.
  3. Cook and stir until browned, about 1 minute. Add tomatoes.
  4. Cook and stir until softened and reduced, about 7 minutes. Stir in chicken.
  5. Cook and stir until it is dark red and sauce thickens and starts to stick to the pan, 10 to 15 minutes.
